Dampukht

Dampukht is a cooking technique in which meat and vegetables are cooked over a low flame, generally in dough-sealed contaiers with few spices.

    Ingredients

  • Beef-2kg
  • Potatoes-1kg
  • Tomatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Onion
  • Lemons
  • Garlic-1 whole
  • Green Chillies-12
  • Salt

    Method of Cooking

  1. 1 teaspoon salt sprinkling on beef and mix it very well.
  2. 1st Layer: Putting fats in bottom layer. 2nd layer add potatoes, garlic and ginger in above the fat.
  3. 3rd Layer: Beef are kept above it.
  4. In the last stage add 8-9 green chillies and sprinkle 5 teaspoon lemon sauce in beef.
  5. Now you have to keep pot close with dough and cook over slow flame for 1 hour.
  6. Now your dampukht is cooked
